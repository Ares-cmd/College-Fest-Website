ABOUT INNOVISION 2K20

For the past 9 years INNOVISION has only a single motive ,to promote the students in every feild possible.This year also we are conducting severel competitions in different branches i.e,TECHNICAL,CULTURAL,NON-TECH & SPORTS.

CULTURAL

"CULTURAL FEST at IEC is a platform where students from every clollege gather together to enjoy and celebrate the different cultures from all around the Globe".

TECHINAL

"TECHNICAL FEST at IEC aims to polsih the future technocrats who aim to be at the apex of the tecnical World".

NON-TECHNICAL

"NON-TECHNICAL FEST holds the idea for pushing the students to show their communication skills,boost their confidence , prepare for their interviews and develop a dynamic personality ".

SPORTS

"SPORTS are a very necessary for overall development of a human being.INDIA is following a campaighn of KHELO INDIA,Thus SPORTS FEST are a crucial part of techno-cultural fest".

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the INNOVISION 2K20?

INNOVISION 2K20 is the annual techno-cultural and sports fest of the IEC-CET, it is organized every year on the IEC campus for the students from every college.

How do I register in the INNOVISION?

Registration in INNOVISION is very simple and easy. It can be done through the website(iecinnovision2k20.com) or (offline at IEC helpdesk).

When will INNOVISION officially start?

The inauguration date of the event is 26th February, it has a period of 2 days and it will end on 27th February 2020.

What are the events in the INNOVISION 2k20?

There are a total of 37 events in the fest, however, the detailed list has been provided on the website.

What are the entry fees for any event at INNOVISION?

The fees for every event is given on the website, however, for solo events, it’s 100 INR while for group events its 50 INR, fees for sports events are separate.

Is there any cash prize for the winners?

Yes ,there is a cash prize for the winners and runner-ups along with the certificates, there are certificates for all the participants as well.
